H — P&L ATTRIBUTION — CHEAT SHEET

Quick reference for Greek-based and factor-based P&L decomposition, patterns, P&L explain, and fraud detection

THE MASTER EQUATION

$$\text{Total P\&L} = \Delta \text{P\&L} + \Gamma \text{P\&L} + \Theta \text{P\&L} + \mathcal{V} \text{P\&L} + \rho \text{P\&L} + \text{Cross} + \text{Unexplained}$$

Component Formula What it measures
Delta Σ δ_i × ΔS_i Directional bet — was the PM right?
Gamma ½ Σ γ_i × (ΔS_i)² Convexity payoff — always positive for long γ
Theta Σ θ_i × Δt Time decay — rent for holding options
Vega Σ ν_i × ΔIV_i Vol change — is PM secretly a vol trader?
Rho Σ ρ_i × Δr Rate impact — small for short-dated, big for FI
Cross Vanna, charm, volga, speed Normally <5% of total. 20-30% in crises.
Unexplained Actual − Attributed Red flag if >5%. Investigate immediately if >15%.

Theta-gamma breakeven: $\Delta S_{BE} = \sqrt{2|\theta|/\gamma}$ — move needed for gamma to offset theta.

GREEK P&L RATIO DIAGNOSTICS

Ratio What it reveals Red flag
Vega % of total Vol trader or directional? >50% → PM is trading vol, not stocks
Theta % of total How much is carry income? >70% consistently → short gamma collector
Gamma / Theta (cumulative) Fair rent for gamma? <0.5 → overpaying for protection
Cumulative theta / Tail loss Pennies-vs-steamroller check <0.1 → earning $2M to risk $20M
Delta % of total Pure directional bettor? >90% → just riding beta
Factor / Total Alpha or factor exposure? >80% → delivering factor, not skill
Unexplained / Total Model working? >15% → escalate
Cross-effects / Total Higher-order risk material? >20% → crisis regime, need full repricing

Quick diagnosis: Theta = 75% of P&L → short gamma collector (Sharpe looks great, tail will destroy). Vega = 87% of P&L → vol trader who might not know it.

FACTOR-BASED ATTRIBUTION

$$R_p = \alpha + \sum_k \beta_k f_k + \epsilon$$

Layer What it answers
Greek Which INSTRUMENTS drove P&L?
Factor Which BETS drove P&L? (market, momentum, rates...)
Alpha vs factor Is the PM skilled or just delivering factor exposure?
Carry vs active Passive income vs active trading decisions?

Brinson (long-only): Allocation effect + Selection effect + Interaction effect.

Rates desk: P&L = Carry + Rolldown + Duration + Curve + Spread + Convexity + Residual.

SHORT GAMMA P&L PROFILE (CRITICAL)

Period Daily P&L Cumulative
Day 1-19 (calm) +$130K/day (theta dominates) +$2,470K
Day 20 (SPX −4%, VIX +12) −$18,850K (gamma + vega) −$16,380K

19 good days wiped out + more by 1 bad day. Sharpe was 3.0+ before day 20.

Detection: Theta P&L consistently >80% of daily P&L → PM is short gamma/vol.

Management: Track cumulative theta vs worst-case tail loss. "$2M theta collected vs $18M tail loss = 9:1 risk/reward AGAINST."

6 P&L PATTERNS TO RECOGNIZE

Pattern Signature RM Action
Accidental vol trader 60%+ of P&L from vega, not delta "Is vol the trade you intended?"
Theta bleed −$80K/day theta drag "Need $80K/day from delta/gamma just to break even"
Short gamma collector Small daily gains, then massive single-day loss Track theta collected vs tail loss exposure
Unexplained growing 2% → 5% → 12% across months Escalate. Model is deteriorating.
Factor dress-up 90% of "alpha" is actually momentum factor "R² is 0.90 against momentum. IR on true alpha = 0.15"
Basis drift Hedge effectiveness R² falling (0.93 → 0.78) "Adjust hedge ratio or find better instrument"

P&L EXPLAIN — MODEL VALIDATION

$$\text{P\&L Explain Ratio} = \frac{\text{Predicted P\&L (from Greeks)}}{\text{Actual P\&L}}$$

Ratio Quality Action
0.95-1.05 Excellent No action
0.80-0.95 Acceptable Monitor
0.60-0.80 Poor Formal review
<0.60 Failed Model is wrong — escalate

Gap patterns:
- Always positive → conservative Greeks (capital inefficiency)
- Always negative → underestimates losses → DANGEROUS
- Spikes on big-move days → higher-order terms missing → need full repricing
- On specific names → wrong pricing model for those positions

UNEXPLAINED P&L — RED FLAGS

Level Action
<2% Normal noise
2-5% Investigate: prices, marks, missing Greeks
5-15% Formal investigation: missing risk factor? Model error?
>15% Escalate immediately. Possible fraud, hidden positions, rogue trading.

Common causes: Stale marks, wrong Greeks, missing risk factor, gap risk, corporate actions, discrete vs continuous math.

FRAUD DETECTION VIA P&L ATTRIBUTION

Red flag What it might mean
Profit with NO risk exposures Fabricated trades (Barings)
Unexplained always positive Marks being smoothed (London Whale)
Big positions but flat P&L Fictitious offsetting trades (SocGen)
One illiquid name dominates P&L Mark manipulation on OTC

INTERVIEW QUICK-FIRE

"Explain a PM's daily P&L?" → "Two dimensions: Greek-based (delta = direction, gamma = convexity, theta = time, vega = vol) and factor-based (market, sector, style, macro → residual alpha). If PM thinks they're trading direction but 60% is vega → they're a vol trader. If 90% is momentum factor → they're selling factor at alpha prices. Unexplained >5% → model is missing something or worse."

"Unexplained P&L keeps growing?" → "Escalating red flag. Quantify (2% = noise, 15% = broken model). Check direction (always negative = DANGEROUS). Diagnose: stale prices? Wrong Greeks? Missing risk factor? If it persists after data/calibration checks → model whose VaR and stress tests should not be trusted."

"Short gamma — how to identify?" → "P&L signature: consistent positive theta, near-zero gamma P&L on calm days, then massive loss on big-move day. Sharpe looks great pre-blowup. Track cumulative theta vs worst-case tail. If $2M theta vs $20M tail loss → 10:1 against. Ensure drawdown limits can absorb the tail."

"Alpha vs factor?" → "Multi-factor regression. R² shows how much is explained. Alpha intercept needs t-stat >2.0 + 3-4 years of data. Plot cumulative alpha vs factor over time — flat alpha + rising total = pure factor delivery."
