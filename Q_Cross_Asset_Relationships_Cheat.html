Q — CROSS-ASSET RELATIONSHIPS — CHEAT SHEET

5 Transmission Chains

  1. Oil → Inflation → Rates → Equities → Credit → EM (hours to months)
  2. Rate Shock → Duration → FX → EM Contagion ("dollar wrecking ball")
  3. Risk-Off Cascade → VIX spike → margin calls → forced selling → correlation → 1.0 → liquidity crisis
  4. Carry Trade Unwind → borrow JPY/CHF → invest HY/EM → vol spike → self-reinforcing unwind
  5. Credit Impulse → Real Economy Feedback → tighter credit → less investment → weaker GDP → more defaults

Speed Calibration

Leg Time
Oil → breakevens Minutes
CPI → Fed repricing Same day
Equity weakness → credit 1-5 days
Credit tightening → real economy 3-12 months

Stock-Bond Correlation (THE Master Variable)

  • Growth-dominated risk → ρ < 0 → bonds hedge equities → 60/40 works → VaR accurate
  • Inflation-dominated risk → ρ > 0 → bonds amplify losses → 60/40 fails → VaR UNDERSTATES
  • Track rolling 21d & 63d SPX/TLT correlation. When sign flips → reassess ALL hedge ratios

Conditional Correlation

  • VIX < 20: ρ(SPX,HY) ≈ 0.40
  • VIX > 40: ρ(SPX,HY) ≈ 0.85 — diversification mostly gone
  • Crisis VaR = 1.5-2.5× normal VaR due to correlation spike

Safe Haven Hierarchy

  1. T-bills (< 3M) — most reliable

  3. Gold — fails in dash-for-cash / real rate spikes
  4. JPY, CHF — fails on CB policy shifts
  5. USD — fails when US IS the problem

Narrative Regime

  • "Good news is bad news": Strong NFP → equities DOWN (Fed won't cut)
  • "Growth scare": Strong NFP → equities UP (no recession!)
  • How to ID: Check if SPX & UST move SAME direction (growth) or OPPOSITE (Fed/inflation)

Dealer Gamma

  • Short gamma: dealers sell into weakness, buy into strength → AMPLIFIES moves
  • Long gamma: dealers buy into weakness, sell into strength → DAMPENS moves
  • 0DTE options ≈ 50% of SPX volume → intraday gamma regimes

Systematic Strategy Flows

  • CTAs: Trend-following → accelerate momentum, violent on reversal
  • Risk parity: Deleverage when vol rises → sells BOTH stocks AND bonds
  • Vol targeting: Reduces exposure when realized vol rises → pro-cyclical selling

Contagion Channels

  1. Information (seconds) — news reprices globally
  2. Portfolio balance (hours-days) — losses → sell other assets
  3. Funding (days-weeks) — lender pulls credit broadly
  4. Wake-up call (days-weeks) — reprices all "similar" exposures
  5. Margin/collateral (hours-days) — loss → margin call → forced selling → spiral
