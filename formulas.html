Greeks & Options

Delta

  • Definition: ∂V/∂S — change in option value per $1 move in underlying
  • Range: Long Call 0→+1 | Long Put −1→0 | Short = opposite
  • Dollar delta = Δ × contracts × multiplier × spot

Gamma

  • Definition: ∂²V/∂S² = ∂Δ/∂S — rate of change of delta
  • Dollar gamma P&L: ½ × Γ$ × (%ΔS)²
  • Theta acceleration: Θ ∝ 1/√T

Theta

  • Definition: ∂V/∂t — daily rent for holding optionality ($/day)
  • Long options = −Θ (pay rent) | Short options = +Θ (earn rent)
Days to Expiry Decay Rate (vs 90d baseline)
30 1.7×
7 3.6×
1 9.5×

Vega

  • Definition: ∂V/∂σ — change in option value per 1 vol point
  • Long options = +ν | Short options = −ν

GARCH(1,1)

$$\sigma_t^2 = \omega + \alpha\,r_{t-1}^2 + \beta\,\sigma_{t-1}^2$$

  • Persistence = α + β (typical: 0.95–0.99)

  • Half-life = −ln(2)/ln(α+β)

Portfolio & Hedging

Correlation

$$\rho = \frac{\text{Cov}(X,Y)}{\sigma_X \times \sigma_Y}$$

Portfolio Vol

$$\sigma_p = \sigma\sqrt{\frac{1}{N} + \frac{N-1}{N} \times \bar{\rho}}$$

Floor = σ√ρ̄ (can never diversify below this)

Diversification Ratio

$$DR = \frac{\Sigma(w_i \times \sigma_i)}{\sigma_p}$$

DR → 1.0 = no diversification | DR > 1.5 = good

Concentration (HHI)

$$HHI = \sum w_i^2 \qquad \text{Effective N} = 1/HHI$$

Hedge Effectiveness

$$R^2 = 1 - \frac{\text{Var(hedged)}}{\text{Var(unhedged)}}$$

R² > 0.95 = excellent | <0.50 = not a hedge

Optimal Hedge Ratio

$$h^* = \rho \times \frac{\sigma_{\text{position}}}{\sigma_{\text{hedge}}}$$

Beta Hedge Sizing

$$N = \frac{V_p \times (\beta_{current} - \beta_{target})}{V_f}$$

Kelly Criterion

$$f^* = \frac{p \cdot b - q}{b}$$

In practice: use ¼ or ½ Kelly

Factor Model

$$r_i = \alpha + \sum \beta_{ik}\,f_k + \epsilon_i$$

$$\text{Factor}k\,\% = \frac{\beta{pk}^2 \times \sigma_k^2}{\sigma_p^2}$$

Component VaR

$$\text{CVaR}i = w_i \times \beta{i,p} \times \text{VaR}_p$$

Market Impact (Almgren-Chriss)

$$\text{Cost} = \tfrac{1}{2}\,\text{spread} + \eta \times \text{speed} + \gamma \times \frac{Q}{\text{ADV}}$$

Non-linear: 5× size costs 10–15× more
