PART 4: PROBE DEFENSE — KEY CLAIMS

SOFR Skew Scanner

"What is skew?"
Implied vol pattern across strikes. Equity indices = puts more expensive (negative skew) because downside is feared. Rates = depends on environment. Skew shows where the market sees risk + where hedging demand creates imbalance.

"Why focus on OI, delta, gamma?"
OI shows strike concentration. Delta shows directional exposure. Gamma shows sensitivity. Strikes with high OI + high gamma = where dealers are concentrated. Hedging activity at those strikes amplifies moves. It's a fault-line map.

"What's net short gamma?"
Dealers short options at a strike. As underlying approaches that strike, delta changes rapidly — they're forced to buy on up-moves and sell on down-moves to re-hedge. Creates a feedback loop that amplifies direction. Net long gamma does the opposite — creates mean reversion.

Fire Drill

"Walk me through a fire drill"
Phase 1: Risk assessment — map all positions, identify most illiquid/correlated.
Phase 2: Counterparty coordination — contact 9 banks/corporates, agree on sequence.
Phase 3: Execution simulation — validate liquidation within regulatory timelines, test CCP margins.
Result: <0.2% drag, identified 2 operational gaps, fixed preemptively.

"Why natural gas short power?"
Most illiquid + correlated combo. Gas-fired plants set electricity marginal price — long gas/short power is right-way correlated normally but hardest to unwind. Power market has limited liquidity, seasonal contracts, basis risk. We chose it TO FIND PROBLEMS.

Natixis (Keep SHORT)

SABR Calibration
Four parameters: α (vol level), β (CEV backbone — fixed 0.5 for rates, ~1.0 equities), ρ (skew), ν (smile). Calibrate using Levenberg-Marquardt minimizing squared diff between model + market implied vols. 15bps improvement: legacy 45bps RMSE → SABR 30bps.

Gold-UST Decoupling
Historically -0.3 to -0.5 correlation (yields down, gold up). Turned positive Q4 2025 = 3-sigma event. Root cause: China/Russia/Middle East central bank gold buying (de-dollarization). Chow breakpoint test confirmed structural break. Adjusted hedges → 15% drawdown reduction.
