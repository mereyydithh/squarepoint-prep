THE FIVE TRADEOFFS (EVERY HEDGE HAS A PRICE)

Tradeoff Tension PM question
Cost vs protection Better protection = more premium "How much drag can your Sharpe absorb?"
Precision vs liquidity Single-name = perfect but expensive; Index = cheap but basis risk "Exact coverage or directional protection?"
Horizon mismatch Position = no expiry; Hedge = expires вҶ’ roll costs "Event (1 week) or regime (6 months)?"
Upside surrender Collars/call overwriting cap gains "Max return you'd accept for zero premium?"
Timing Cheapest when you don't need it; most expensive when you do "Pay insurance when the sun is shining?"

DECISION FRAMEWORK вҖ” 5 QUESTIONS

  1. What SPECIFIC risk? вҶ’ Delta, vega, gamma, DV01, credit, concentration, tail, basis, liquidity
  2. What horizon? вҶ’ 1-5 days (weekly options) | 1-4 weeks (monthlies) | 1-6 months (quarterlies) | 6M+ (LEAPs/rolling)
  3. Upside tolerance? вҶ’ None (puts) | Capped (collar) | Partially capped (put spread)
  4. Premium budget? вҶ’ Max ~15% of expected alpha for hedge cost
  5. Basis risk accepted? вҶ’ RВІ > 0.90 (excellent) | 0.70-0.90 (good) | < 0.70 (reconsider instrument)

KEY FORMULAS

Beta hedge sizing:
$$N = \frac{V_p \times (\beta_{\text{current}} - \beta_{\text{target}})}{V_f}$$

Optimal hedge ratio:
$$h^* = \rho \times \frac{\sigma_{\text{position}}}{\sigma_{\text{hedge}}}$$

Hedge effectiveness:
$$R^2 = 1 - \frac{\text{Var(hedged)}}{\text{Var(unhedged)}}$$

Delta-hedged P&L:
$$\text{P\&L} = \frac{1}{2}\Gamma(\Delta S)^2 - \Theta \Delta t$$

Breakeven daily move:
$$\Delta S_{\text{BE}} = \sigma_{\text{implied}} \times S \times \sqrt{\Delta t}$$

Tail hedge EV:
$$\text{EV} = P(\text{tail}) \times \text{Payoff} - (1 - P(\text{tail})) \times \text{Annual cost}$$

COMMON MISTAKES

  1. Hedging too late вҶ’ Buy when VIX is LOW, not after the sell-off
  2. Over-hedging вҶ’ Theta drag > alpha = portfolio can't make money
  3. Wrong instrument вҶ’ RВІ < 0.70 = find a better instrument
  4. Set-and-forget вҶ’ Roll calendar with alerts, no coverage gaps
  5. Ignoring roll costs вҶ’ Monthly puts = 3%+ annualized drag
  6. "Free" collar illusion вҶ’ Quantify upside surrender in DOLLARS
  7. Wrong Greek вҶ’ Futures = delta only; puts = delta + gamma + vega
  8. Correlation-dependent вҶ’ Monitor ПҒ weekly, have Plan B

INTERVIEW ANSWER TEMPLATES

"Hedge concentrated equity?"
вҶ’ Framework first (5 questions) вҶ’ Single-name put (cleanest, expensive) вҶ’ Put spread (halves cost, caps protection) вҶ’ Collar (zero cost, caps upside) вҶ’ Futures (free but basis risk, RВІ вүҲ 0.6 for single name vs index) вҶ’ Make tradeoff explicit to PM

"When NOT to hedge?"
вҶ’ (1) Cost > risk: hedge EV is negative вҶ’ (2) Hedge adds MORE risk via fragile correlation вҶ’ (3) PM's thesis IS the risk and it's within limits

"Puts vs futures?"
вҶ’ Futures: free, liquid, delta-only, linear, no gap protection
вҶ’ Puts: premium cost, delta + gamma + vega, convex, GAP protection, hedge IMPROVES as crash deepens

"How evaluate hedge effectiveness?"
вҶ’ RВІ overall + RВІ_stress on tail days вҶ’ If RВІ_stress << RВІ_overall, hedge gives false confidence
