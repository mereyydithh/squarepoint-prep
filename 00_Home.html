STUDY GUIDE — JUNIOR RISK MANAGER, DISCRETIONARY TEAM

Squarepoint Capital — Complete Knowledge Map

HOW TO USE THIS

This is your study system. 25 domains across 8 levels, organized by dependency — each level builds on the one before it. Links point to either existing prep files (in the parent folder) or new domain files (in this folder).

Depth tiers are marked on each domain:
- 🔴 DEEP — You will be tested hard. Know cold, explain fluently, apply under pressure.
- 🟡 WELL — Confident conversation. Can explain clearly, answer follow-ups, give examples.
- 🟢 AWARE — Know it exists, what it does, and why. 2-3 sentences max.

INTEGRATION TEST

Once you've studied all domains, you should be able to handle this in 90 seconds without notes:

"I'm long $80M in a single energy name, short $30M in index puts, and oil just spiked 8%. Walk me through what I should be thinking about."

That requires: A (commodities), D (delta/gamma on the short puts), G (concentration + correlation), I (is the index put acting as a hedge or adding risk?), S (prioritize what to flag first), and U (scenario: what if oil goes to +15%?) — all at once, in real time.

If you can do that, you're ready.
